"WrngvRight" A Sexual Consent video, Installation w/ Digital Video, 2:23min

WrngvRight" A Sexual Consent video:

Not everybody flows the same flow, some might want to go slow, and others kick it off real fast. No matter the relationship, consent can be given or taken away at anytime from your partner. Consent is part of ones trust barrier which can easily be physically or mentally scarred when in a wrong relationship or even when looking for future relationships. This video was intended to make the viewer feel uncomfortable. Noises in the beginning of the video are forced and aggressive in contrast to the mellow and gentleman like tone of speech at the end of the video. The installation is placed in a corner to create the feeling of being trapped/enclosed. It begins w/ pictures of mother earth and nature as a woman desperately calls for help, once she is told to put on her dress, a revelation begins, drifting her into space as a gentleman proceeds to asking for her consent to continue when everything's already done. Contrast is shown as one is simply forced in one situation, while in the other the man is hesitant to continue. This comparison between both scenarios creates a sense of what is wrong and right through the viewers sense of judgement.
