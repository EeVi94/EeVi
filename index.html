About Me:

Evan Villaflor's CV

Through Digital Media I was able to create and explore deeper and meaningful Artworks versus what I was able to accomplish with Acylic and Graphite. Within my various artworks I have been exploring themes of emotion and the different hardships that one may come across or experience within their lifetime (Weight Gain/Loss, Communication with Specific Others, Overload in work, Depression, Anxiety etc). Most of my work will include Words, Repetition, Glitches, 3-D Models, Yin and Yang balance, and subject matter that people usually don't talk about. I express them through Digital media Art and Video. Influenced by artpieces from Roy Lichtenstein, Barbara Kruger, Andy Warhol, Audrey Flack, and Frida Kahlo. Designs from different cartoons, comics, video games, manga, animations, animals and other culture/identities. You'll either find straight sobering thoughts or warm filled joy/comedy in my artwork as I try to relate to as many people as possible through this connection we all now have Online. I want everyone to feel the emotions and experiences I have had, living here in California.
